WorkProof

Verifiable proof of work for the AI-augmented workforce — receipts that managers, auditors, and teammates can actually trust.

The Problem

In a world where anyone can ship code, copy, and decks in minutes, "work" no longer proves work was done.

  • Output volume has decoupled from effort, expertise, and accountability.
  • Managers can't tell genuine craft from a five-minute prompt.
  • Reviewers, auditors, and clients have no signal that survives the AI flood.
  • Trust in deliverables is collapsing right when stakes are rising.

Our Solution

WorkProof captures cryptographically signed, replayable receipts of how work actually got made.

  • Lightweight agent records intent, edits, prompts, and decisions in the background.
  • Every session is hashed, timestamped, and signed — tamper-evident by design.
  • Shareable proof links: a verifier sees the story, not the secrets.
  • Privacy-first: redaction is local, disclosure is opt-in, granular, and revocable.

Why It Matters

Proof of work becomes the new currency of professional trust.

  • Hiring & freelance: portfolios you can actually verify, not just claim.
  • Enterprise: audit trails for AI-assisted decisions and regulated workflows.
  • Education: assessments that reward process, not just polished output.
  • Open standard: portable receipts across tools, employers, and platforms.
