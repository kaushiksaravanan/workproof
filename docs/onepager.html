WorkProof

Verifiable proof of work for the AI-augmented workforce — receipts that managers, auditors, and teammates can actually trust.

The Problem

Contractors and crews finish jobs every day and can't prove it a week later — and $40B in trade-industry payments stall in disputes because there's no verifiable record.

  • Photos scatter across camera rolls, WhatsApp threads, and lost text messages.
  • A photo alone isn't proof: it has no timestamp, no location, no signature.
  • Customer disputes drag out final payment; crews eat the loss.
  • Existing tools require cloud logins that field workers won't use.

Our Solution

A 30-second capture flow on the crew's phone that turns each finished job into a hashed, anchored, portable proof.

  • Photo + voice memo + location + timestamp, captured in one flow.
  • Editable transcript on-device — no cloud upload of raw audio. Regex extractor pulls work type / client / amounts from the typed text (offline-first; the Gemini path exists in code but is currently disabled in the app for privacy).
  • The bundle is SHA-256 hashed (canonical record + photo bytes + audio bytes); the hash is anchored on Polygon Amoy via ethers.js for permanent timestamping.
  • Exported as a one-page PDF with the content hash + Polygonscan link — travels through WhatsApp, email, SMS.

Why It Matters

A tamper-evident receipt that the crew already knows how to send.

  • Trades & construction: paint, plumbing, roofing, wiring — dispute-proof the invoice.
  • Property management: proof of maintenance for absentee owners and compliance audits.
  • Gig platforms: verifiable job completion without giving the platform your camera roll.
  • Zero cloud dependency: works offline; queued proofs anchor when the phone reconnects.
